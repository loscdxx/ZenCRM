About Us

Our Story

ZenCRM was founded in 2020 by a team of software engineers and sales professionals who were frustrated with the complexity and inefficiency of existing CRM solutions. We set out to create a CRM that was intuitive, powerful, and actually enjoyable to use.

Our mission is simple: to help businesses build better relationships with their customers through technology that works for people, not against them.

The ZenCRM leadership team planning our product roadmap

Our Values

Customer First

We believe that the best products are built with users in mind. Every feature we develop starts with the question: "How does this help our customers?"

Innovation

We're constantly pushing the boundaries of what a CRM can do, incorporating the latest technologies to make your work easier and more effective.

Security

Your data is your business's most valuable asset. We employ industry-leading security practices to ensure it stays safe and private.

Passion

We love what we do, and it shows in our product. Our team is passionate about creating software that makes a real difference in how businesses operate.
