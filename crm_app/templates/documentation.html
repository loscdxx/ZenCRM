Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import my existing customer data?

ZenCRM supports CSV imports for customer data. Go to Customers > Import and follow the instructions to map your data fields.

Can I customize the deal stages?

Yes, you can customize deal stages in Settings > Deal Pipeline. You can add, remove, or reorder stages to match your sales process.

How do I add team members to my account?

Go to Settings > Team Members and click "Add Team Member". Enter their email address and select their role to send an invitation.

Is there a mobile app available?

Yes, ZenCRM has mobile apps for both iOS and Android. You can download them from the respective app stores.
